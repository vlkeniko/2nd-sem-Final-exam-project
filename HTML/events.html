Come to Chromosome for the initial wine tasting!


Our new offer will consist of a total of 4 wines; 2 red and 2 white. These wines are to be tasted on May 6th. In both categories, one wine will be on the sweet side and one wine on the dry side. Common to all four is that the beautiful and talented Tola will serve and tell them.

Tola has many years of experience in the wine industry and works daily as a salesman / bartender at Vin og Petanque in Aarhus. So you are in safe hands as you guide yourself through the four fine wine tastings that await you!

So come fresh, tired, thirsty - come as you are - and wait for a cozy evening with good wine and at least as good company. (... and if one or more wines are tasted with another glass, it is well covered. When the tasting is over, the wines can be bought, either for a glass or a whole bottle). Buy your ticket well in advance - only 40 will be sold!

Admission: 150 Kr.
