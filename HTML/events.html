BÆNCH CONCERT



BÆNCH is a new and upcoming Rock Band from Horsens with roots in the psychedelic and progressive sound of the 70s. The Band’s sound is inspired by genres such as Post-Punk, Indie-Rock and Noise-Rock with a clear vision of mixing sounds and ideas into the universe from the band members’ common thoughts. A collective frustration over today’s passive way of thinking and acting led the group together to write their debut album.
